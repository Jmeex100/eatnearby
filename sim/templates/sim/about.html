About EatNearby

Welcome to Eat Nearby ‚Äì your digital bridge between hungry customers and local restaurants. We are a web-based food ordering platform focused on connecting diners with nearby eateries while fostering community-driven engagement.

Inspired by the vibrant food scene around Evelyn Hone College, Eat Nearby was created to simplify food ordering, enhance customer loyalty, and provide clear, accessible information about local dining options.

Our system supports mobile-friendly access, AI-powered restaurant recommendations, and multiple payment options including Cash, Mobile Money, and cards. With real-time location mapping and user reviews, Eat Nearby is redefining the dining experience.
